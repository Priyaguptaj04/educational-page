Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions

How do I enroll in a course?

You can enroll in any course by clicking the "Enroll Now" button on the course page. You'll need to create an account or log in if you already have one. Payment can be made securely through our platform.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept all major credit cards (Visa, MasterCard, American Express), PayPal, and in some regions, bank transfers. All payments are processed securely through our payment gateway partners.

Can I get a refund if I'm not satisfied?

Yes! We offer a 30-day money-back guarantee for all courses. If you're not satisfied with your purchase, simply contact our support team within 30 days of enrollment for a full refund.

Do you offer certificates for completed courses?

Yes, upon successful completion of any course (including passing all assessments), you'll receive a digital certificate that you can download, print, or share on LinkedIn and other professional networks.
